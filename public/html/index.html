Description

Newly built condo only 5 miles south of downtown Moab. Close to Arches and Canyonlands National Parks, Dead Horse State Park, the Sand Flats Recreational Area area, as well as numerous other amazing outdoor areas.

There are 3 bedrooms; one with a king bed, and 2 with queen beds. There is also a queen sized pull out sofa, as well as 2 cots, and a pack 'n play. There are two bathrooms, a laundry room with washer and dryer, and a fully furnished kitchen. The patio has views of the Moab Rim and has a grill and patio furniture. In the living room there is also an electric fireplace that adds ambiance for those cooler nights in Moab.

Guests have use of the whole house. The owner lives off site, but in town.

I live in town and will have my number available if guests need anything. Occasionally, I will be out of town and unreachable, in which case I will have an alternate emergency number available to guests.

A car is needed to see the sights in and around Moab.

This unit is on the second story and there is no elevator. Being on the 2nd floor means that the views from the balcony are better!

There are 2 smart TVs in the house (living room and master bedroom). A netflix account is provided.

A Pack 'n Play is provided in the event that you are bringing an infant.

A one time $75 cleaning fee is included on every reservation.

The 10.5% room tax that all nightly rentals are required to collect is included in the price.
